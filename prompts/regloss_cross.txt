2. For each part: a "homeLang" gloss and a "homeLangDetails" explanation.

- 1 word ideally, 2 at most — imagine it printed on a small game tile

- Roots: use the single clearest <HOMELANG> word for that meaning
- Use the English gloss as a guide, but adapt naturally for <HOMELANG> speakers

Rules for homeLangDetails (explanatory notes):
- 1–2 sentences in <HOMELANG> explaining the morpheme's meaning and use

- Mention 1–2 other <TARGETLANG> words that use the same morpheme, to help the learner recognize it elsewhere
- For grammatical affixes, briefly explain what role they play
